## Section Order & Content

### Section 1: Case Study Header

**Format:** Italic subtitle line beneath Heading 1

```
Case Study #[NN]: [Descriptive Title]
[Customer Type] | [Source Host] → [Destination Host] | Outcome: [Result]
```

**Descriptive title guidelines:**
- Capture the defining characteristic of the case
- Examples: "The Frustrated DIY Customer", "The Deadline-Driven eCommerce Migration", "The Restricted Host Recovery"

### Section 3: The Technical Challenge

**Opening paragraph:** Brief summary of how many tickets and what the overall technical situation was.

**For each ticket / technical event:**

Sub-heading: `Ticket #[XXXXX] — [Brief Label]`

Body text describing what the customer attempted and what went wrong.

**Error callout box** (warning style — yellow):
```
Label: "Error Encountered by Customer:"
Text: [Exact error message, sanitized of credential paths if needed]
```

**Root Cause Analysis** (bold label + bullets):
- Bullet 1: What caused it
- Bullet 2: Why this host / environment made it happen
- Bullet 3: Customer context (experience level, what they knew)

**Resolution section** (if applicable at this stage):
- Bullet list of resolution steps

### Section 8: Actionable Takeaways

**Format:** Bullet list — 5-8 concrete, prescriptive action items.

Each bullet should be a complete sentence starting with a verb or "When/If/Always/Never":
- "When a customer hits installer errors on [Host] — think [issue type] FIRST."
- "Always embed the WGMS upsell at the bottom of complex troubleshooting replies."
- "Use WGMS30 when a customer hesitates due to prior money spent."
